Welcome to Yoga with Sue

Yoga Classes

Students of all ages and abilities are welcome at Sue's classes and if you need more details, please call Sue and she would be very pleased to help you. For comfort, please wear loose clothing that can stretch as you do and bring a yoga/exercise mat too.

The classes are "pay-as-you-go" at £5 per one hour session and seniors get a reduced rate of £4.50

Sue holds classes in Sutton Coldfield, Shenstone and Great Barr so you can choose the one that is most convenient for you. Ring or text 07743 028383 for more information. Please state your address by text or voicemail if you would like venues and times information posted to you.

About Sue

Sue Arnold is a fully qualified instructor and is registered with the British Wheel of Yoga. She has been teaching yoga classes for more than 10 years.
